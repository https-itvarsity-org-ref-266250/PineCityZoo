LIONS

The lion(Panthena leo)is a large cat of the genius Panthera native to Africa and India. It has a mascular, broad-chested body;short, rounded head; rounnd ears , and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.

It is sexually dormophic;adult male lions are larger than female lions and have a prominent mane.It is a social species, forming groups called prides.A lions pride's consists of a few adult males, related males, females and cubs.Groups of females lions usually hunt together, preying mostly on large ungulates.

The lion is an apex and keystone predator ,although some lion scavenge when opportunities occur and have been know to hunt humans, lions typically do not actively seek out and prey on humans.
